# Executive Summary

Ambience ExpenseFlow is delivered as a cloud-based Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) platform that enables organizations to automate travel and expense management while improving compliance, audit readiness, and financial visibility.

Customers subscribe to the platform through recurring monthly or annual licensing plans based on organization size, user count, and feature requirements.

The long-term objective is to evolve from an expense management application into a comprehensive AI-powered Financial Operations Platform.
