# Executive Summary

Customer Personas define the types of organizations that purchase Ambience ExpenseFlow. These personas guide product strategy, pricing, marketing, sales, implementation, and long-term roadmap planning.

Unlike User Personas, which describe individual users, Customer Personas describe the organizations that make purchasing decisions.

---

# Persona 1 – Small Business

## Organization Profile

- Employees: 10–50
- Finance Team: 1–3 people
- Annual Revenue: <$10M
- Limited IT resources

### Business Challenges

- Manual expense tracking
- Spreadsheet-based reimbursements
- Lost receipts
- Slow approvals

### Buying Priorities

- Ease of use
- Low implementation effort
- Affordable pricing
- Fast deployment

### Primary Decision Maker

- Business Owner
- Office Manager
- Accountant

### Product Fit

- Starter Edition
