# Executive Summary

Ambience ExpenseFlow follows a Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) subscription model designed to support organizations ranging from small businesses to global enterprises. Pricing is transparent, scalable, and aligned with customer growth.

The licensing strategy balances affordability for smaller organizations with advanced capabilities and support for enterprise customers.

---

# Pricing Principles

Our pricing model is designed to be:

- Transparent
- Predictable
- Scalable
- Feature-based
- Enterprise-friendly
- Easy to understand

Organizations pay based on the edition they choose and the number of licensed users.
