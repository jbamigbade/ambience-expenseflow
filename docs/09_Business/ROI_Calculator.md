# Executive Summary

Organizations invest in expense management software to improve operational efficiency, reduce costs, strengthen financial controls, and enhance employee experience. This document provides a framework for estimating the return on investment (ROI) that customers can expect from implementing Ambience ExpenseFlow.

The calculator is intended for executive decision-makers evaluating the financial impact of replacing manual or legacy expense management processes.

---

# ROI Methodology

The ROI model compares:

- Current operating costs
- Estimated future operating costs with Ambience ExpenseFlow
- Productivity improvements
- Compliance improvements
- Financial savings

ROI is calculated using:

> **ROI (%) = ((Total Benefits − Total Investment) ÷ Total Investment) × 100**

# Sample ROI Scenario 1

## Organization Profile

- Employees: 100
- Expense Reports per Month: 300
- Finance Staff: 3
- Managers: 12

---

### Current Challenges

- Manual approvals
- Spreadsheet tracking
- Lost receipts
- Delayed reimbursements

---

### Estimated Annual Benefits

| Category                     | Estimated Savings |
|------------------------------|-------------------:|
| Labor Efficiency             |     $40,000       |
| Faster Reimbursements        |     $12,000       |
| Reduced Fraud                |      $8,000       |
| Audit Preparation            |     $15,000       |
| Reduced Paper Processing     |      $5,000       |
| Total Estimated Annual Benefit |     **$80,000**   |

---

### Estimated Annual Investment

| Category             | Cost      |
|----------------------|-----------:|
| Software Subscription | $24,000   |
| Implementation       |  $8,000   |
| Training             |  $3,000   |
| Total Investment     | **$35,000** |

---

### Estimated ROI

```
ROI = (($80,000 - $35,000) / $35,000) × 100

ROI ≈ 129%
```

Estimated payback period:

**Approximately 5–6 months**
