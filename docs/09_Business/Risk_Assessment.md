Executive Summary

Risk management is essential to ensuring the long-term success, reliability, and trustworthiness of Ambience ExpenseFlow. This document identifies potential risks across business operations, technology, security, compliance, finance, and customer success, while defining mitigation strategies and governance processes.

Risk Management Objectives
    Protect customer data
    Ensure business continuity
    Reduce financial loss
    Maintain customer trust
    Meet regulatory obligations
    Support sustainable growth
    Improve operational resilience
