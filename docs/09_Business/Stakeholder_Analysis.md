## My recommendation

This document is strong enough to serve as Version 1.0. However, for Version 2.0, I recommend expanding it into a full **Stakeholder Governance Guide** (approximately 75–100 pages). That version could include detailed stakeholder journey maps, governance committees, meeting charters, communication templates, customer advisory board governance, partner governance, implementation governance, and change management procedures. It would become the authoritative governance document for the entire Ambience ExpenseFlow organization as the company grows.
