# Executive Summary

Stakeholders are individuals or organizations that influence, purchase, implement, administer, support, govern, or benefit from Ambience ExpenseFlow.

Understanding stakeholder needs ensures that the product delivers measurable business value while balancing security, usability, compliance, scalability, and customer success.

---

# Stakeholder Classification

Stakeholders are grouped into:

- Executive Leadership
- Business Stakeholders
- Operational Stakeholders
- Technical Stakeholders
- Customer Stakeholders
- External Stakeholders

# Risks

Potential stakeholder risks include:

- Misaligned priorities
- Poor communication
- Delayed decision-making
- Inadequate user adoption
- Regulatory changes

Mitigation strategies:

- Regular stakeholder reviews
- Transparent communication
- Product roadmap updates
- Customer advisory board
- Continuous feedback loops
