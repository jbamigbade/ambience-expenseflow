## 1. One-Line Pitch

Ambience ExpenseFlow is an AI-powered enterprise travel and expense management platform that automates expense reporting, approvals, audit readiness, corporate card reconciliation, and financial compliance.

---

## 2. Problem

Organizations still lose time and money through:

- Manual expense reports
- Delayed approvals
- Missing receipts
- Policy violations
- Weak audit trails
- Corporate card reconciliation gaps
- Limited financial visibility

---

## 3. Solution

Ambience ExpenseFlow provides:

- Multi-line expense reports
- AI-assisted policy review
- Manager approval workflows
- Finance dashboards
- Audit Center
- CSV and Excel exports
- Corporate card reconciliation
- Enterprise security
- Cloud-native deployment
