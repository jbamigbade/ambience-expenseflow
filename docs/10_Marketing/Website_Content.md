# Frequently Asked Questions

## Is Ambience ExpenseFlow cloud-native?

Yes. The platform is designed for Google Cloud using Cloud Run, Firestore, Cloud Storage, Pub/Sub, and Vertex AI.

---

## Does the platform support enterprise approvals?

Yes. Employees, managers, finance teams, auditors, and administrators each have role-based workflows.

---

## Can reports be exported?

Yes. Expense and audit data can be exported to CSV and Excel formats.

---

## Is AI required?

No. AI capabilities enhance policy validation and expense intelligence but core expense management functions remain fully operational without AI assistance.

---

## Is the platform secure?

Security features include encrypted communications, role-based access control, audit logging, secure cloud infrastructure, and modern authentication practices.
