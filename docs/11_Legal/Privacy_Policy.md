# 1. Introduction

Ambience ExpenseFlow ("the Platform") respects the privacy of its users and is committed to protecting personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, stored, and protected while using the platform.

---

# 2. Scope

This policy applies to:

- Employees
- Managers
- Finance personnel
- Auditors
- Administrators
- Organization owners

using Ambience ExpenseFlow.

---

# 3. Information We Collect

## Personal Information

- Name
- Email address
- Organization
- Department
- Role
- User ID

---

## Expense Information

- Expense reports
- Expense categories
- Merchant names
- Business purpose
- Amounts
- Receipts
- Reimbursement details

---

## Technical Information

- Browser type
- Device information
- IP address
- Session information
- Authentication logs
- Error logs

# 4. How We Use Information

Information is used to:

- Authenticate users
- Process expense reports
- Support approval workflows
- Generate audit trails
- Improve platform performance
- Detect fraud
- Provide customer support
- Meet legal obligations

---

# 5. Legal Basis for Processing

Where applicable, processing is based on:

- Contractual necessity
- Legitimate business interests
- Legal obligations
- User consent (where required)

---

# 6. Information Sharing

Ambience ExpenseFlow does not sell personal information.

Information may be shared only with:

- Authorized organization administrators
- Approved service providers
- Cloud infrastructure providers
- Regulatory authorities when legally required

---

# 7. Data Security

Security measures include:

- Encryption in transit (TLS)
- Encryption at rest
- Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)
- Multi-layer authentication
- Audit logging
- Secure cloud infrastructure
- Least privilege access

# 8. Data Retention

Refer to the Data Retention Policy for additional details.
