# Executive Summary

This roadmap outlines the planned evolution of Ambience ExpenseFlow beyond Version 1.0.

The long-term vision is to build an enterprise SaaS platform that combines intelligent automation, AI-powered financial assistance, secure cloud architecture, and enterprise workflow management.

---

# Product Vision

To become one of the most trusted AI-powered Travel and Expense Management platforms for organizations worldwide by combining automation, compliance, transparency, and enterprise-grade security.
