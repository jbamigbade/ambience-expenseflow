# Project Overview

Ambience ExpenseFlow is an enterprise AI-powered Travel and Expense Management platform that demonstrates how intelligent agents can automate complex financial workflows while maintaining human oversight, enterprise security, and regulatory compliance.

Rather than relying on a single AI assistant, the platform coordinates multiple specialized agents responsible for different business functions, including expense intake, receipt validation, policy compliance, approval routing, finance processing, and audit intelligence.

The result is a secure, scalable, cloud-native solution that improves efficiency, reduces manual work, and enhances financial governance.

---

# Business Problem

Organizations frequently struggle with:

- Manual expense reporting
- Slow approval workflows
- Policy violations
- Duplicate expense claims
- Missing receipts
- Limited spending visibility
- Audit preparation challenges
- Corporate card reconciliation

These challenges increase operational costs, delay reimbursements, and introduce compliance risks.

---

# Solution

Ambience ExpenseFlow uses specialized AI agents working together to automate the expense management lifecycle while preserving human decision-making for financial approvals.

The platform combines:

- Google Cloud
- FastAPI
- Firestore
- Vertex AI
- AI-assisted validation
- Enterprise security
- Cloud-native architecture
